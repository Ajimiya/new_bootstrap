BHANGRA

Bhangra is a type of traditional folk dance of Punjab, originating in the Sialkot area of Punjab, Pakistan. It is done in the season of harvesting. According to Manuel (2001), bhangra is especially associated with the vernal Vaisakhi festival.

COSTUMES

Traditional Bhangra costume includes men wearing long silk-buttoned shirts, also known as a kurta which is loose to wear, and lungi or tehmat that is a decorated cloth tied around their waist and jugi a waistcoat with no buttons.
